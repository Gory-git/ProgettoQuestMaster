# Example Story: The Crystal Cave

## Story Lore

You are a brave adventurer seeking the legendary Crystal of Power, hidden deep within the ancient Mystic Cave. According to legend, the crystal grants its bearer immense wisdom and magical abilities.

### Initial State
- You stand at the entrance to the Mystic Cave at dawn
- You carry a torch, a rope, and a worn map
- The cave entrance is dark and foreboding
- A wise old hermit lives nearby in a small hut

### Goal
Retrieve the Crystal of Power and escape the cave safely.

### Challenges and Obstacles

1. **The Dark Tunnels**
   - Multiple paths to choose from
   - Some lead to dead ends, others to danger
   - The torch has limited fuel

2. **The Ancient Guardian**
   - A mystical stone guardian protects the crystal chamber
   - Must answer riddles or find another way past
   - Direct confrontation is dangerous

3. **Environmental Hazards**
   - Unstable cave sections that could collapse
   - Underground river that must be crossed
   - Toxic gases in some areas

4. **The Hermit's Knowledge**
   - Can provide clues about the cave
   - May offer helpful items
   - Requires something in return

### Possible Actions/Choices

- Explore different tunnel branches (north, south, east, west)
- Talk to the hermit for guidance
- Use items (torch, rope, map)
- Solve riddles and puzzles
- Rest to recover energy
- Search for hidden passages
- Cross the underground river
- Approach or avoid the guardian
- Take the crystal
- Return to the entrance

### Branching and Depth
- Minimum 2-4 choices at each step
- Story should take 5-8 steps to complete
- Multiple valid paths to success
- Some choices lead to setbacks but not failure

### Atmosphere
Dark, mysterious, ancient, challenging but not hopeless. The player should feel like a clever explorer making important decisions that affect their journey.
